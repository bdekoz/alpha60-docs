◆ sduration

enum class a60::constants::sduration : uint
strong

Data Duration fineness/resolution.

Cache according to a particular duration: per individual sample, per hour sample block, per day, per week.

Augment according to a particular duration: per individual sample, per hour sample block, per day, per week.

Cumulative caches are also necessary for quick lookup of torrent collection phenomena.

Caches organize the information in the specified duration into quickly-accessed information. This includes total number of peers, seeders, etc.

Allowed durations: sample < hour < day < week < cumulative

Each depends on the unit immediately preceeding it, so cumulative durations require week data caches, and day durations require hour data caches. The one difference is that sample caches are optional, and hour caches do not use them.

Note, each level of duration adds significantly to computational complexity, as the move from hour to sample resolutions will show. Thus, unless video or other output media specifically require it, use no more than hour durations.
